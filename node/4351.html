Re: Trying to figue out Solar with out energy diversion

Submitted by Robert Wall on Wed, 26/03/2014 - 00:03.

Is CT1 going to Node 1, and CT2 to Node 2? Because it looks to me from your input list for 10:1 at the beginning that CT1 ought to be on the grid infeed and CT2 on the PV infeed, yet on line 13 of input 1 that treats CT2 as Grid; and on the input list for 10:2 the first log to feed is PV generated.

According to my understanding, and assuming positive is power generated and power imported:

PV generation is CT1 (pure & simple). It will go negative overnight as the inverter consumes some power in standby.

Power/Energy Sold is the negative part of CT2.

Power/Energy Purchased is the positive part of CT2.

Usage is (CT1 + CT2) It should never go negative.

Assuming CT1 is on the PV infeed and CT2 is on the Grid infeed, then this seems to do what you want (the basics only):

Input configuration: CT1

1 Log to feed  - Generated
2 + input CT2
3 Log to feed  - Use

Input configuration: CT2

1 Allow positive 0
2 Log to feed - Purchased
3 Allow negative 0
4 + input CT2
5 Allow negative 0
6 x -1
7 Log to feed - Sold
