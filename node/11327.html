I understand a genuine 3-phase monitor is under consideration. I have no idea what form it will take.

The emonPi was constrained to 2 current inputs by the size of the enclosure and the desire to retain the 3.5 mm jacks, so a 3-phase emonPi with 3 voltage and 3 current inputs looks a little unlikely.

Meanwhile, you might want to look at this thread: http://openenergymonitor.org/emon/node/11324
