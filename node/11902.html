Re: Can I use EmonTX V3 with the RFXtrx433e module ?

Submitted by Robert Wall on Wed, 30/12/2015 - 18:07.

I cannot see the JeeLib protocol (which is what the emonTx uses) listed on the RFXtrx433e web page. You have two possible approaches: If you know one of the exact protocols of the message that the RFXtrx433e expects and can drive the RFM69CW in a way that produces a message obeying that protocol - or you can find a library that does that for you - then that will give you a solution.

The second possibility is to consider whether the RFXtrx433e can be configured to recognise and interpret the transmission from your emonTx - details of the exact message format are on the JeeLabs website.

If you cannot find an answer to one or the other of those, then I suspect the answer is no, you cannot use it.
