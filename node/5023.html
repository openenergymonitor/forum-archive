Re: Upcoming EDX course on measurement and control

Submitted by dBC on Wed, 11/03/2015 - 21:06.

Nah, edx courses are set up for world wide audiences so you should be fine.  Once each week's course material is up, it's up forever.  The only things that may be time sensitive are assignment deadlines but they're usually at least a week away, so +/- 10 hours shouldn't restrict you too much.  If you just want to "audit" the course (the only free option in this case) then you can even start it months later.  You still get to do the assignments, but there's no concept of handing them in.
