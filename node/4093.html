Re: emonTx or emonTH device limit

Submitted by Robert Wall on Tue, 18/03/2014 - 17:46.

It's 30 - the two end values have dedicated functions. See appendix 2 here: http://openenergymonitor.org/emon/buildingblocks/rfm12b2  for details of our "standard", though it's by no means mandatory.

However, if you have 30 transmitting frequently, you might lose data due to collisions when one transmitter blocks another.
