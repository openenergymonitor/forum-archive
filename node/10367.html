EmonCMS User permission/privilege assignment module

Submitted by Mandela on Sat, 21/03/2015 - 20:16

Hello everyone,

It is now a time to limit/manage the end users' account to access or view some information such as the feed and input in order to not confuse them. I propose that there should be users who can add/remove (register) new users, user who do configuration for graph and other display, user who just view their power consumption and super-admin who oversee the whole system.

In summary:

1) Super-admin- in charge of everything

2) Group admin- in charge of just a particular users in terms of configurations and node ID 

3) End-user - just customers or non-admin

I think it is possible to emoncms programmers to build such a wonderful module.
