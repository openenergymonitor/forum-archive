Re: USB cable emonTX V3

Submitted by pb66 on Sun, 13/07/2014 - 18:26.

Troy - The device you have linked to isn't really suitable as it doesn't provide power, it has 4 pins GND, RST TX, RX, the order of those 4 pins do not match the pins on the emonTx's either. If you were able to "patch" the 4 connections to the emonTx to remap them, you could probably solder a fly lead onto the devices PCB to pick up 5v from the mini-usb socket, that could provide 5v to a 5th pin on the emonTx. Although this could get you out of a bind for programming it's not ideal for a fulltime solution.
