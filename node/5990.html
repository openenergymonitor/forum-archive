I can't tell from your post which revision of emoncms you are using, there was a similar issue found with exporting data that was due to the timezone value not being set. The default of "Nan" has since been replaced with "0".

When functioning correctly (depending on browser settings) a new page will open momentarily while the "download to" box is displayed, and then closes again after download is complete. Are downloads blocked by browser maybe?

emonhub is responsible for getting the data to emoncms, then emoncms does the exporting. So you may have better luck with assistance by removing "emonhub" from the title.
