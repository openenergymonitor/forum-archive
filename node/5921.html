Re: Thermal Comfort Visualization ?

Submitted by Eric_AMANN on Mon, 10/11/2014 - 20:17.

Hi,

Attached is a first draft.

I named it "comfort module" but keep in mind that the internal temperature and the relative humidity are only two of the six parameter involved in the thermal comfort : air temperature, mean radiant temperature, air speed, humidity, metabolic rate, and clothing level.

When using those XY graphs that only deals with temp and humidity, you must choose a comfort zone that take into account the others 4 parameters. I put 3 pre-defined comfort zones that corresponds to some standards : ASHRAE (US) and COSTIC (FR). One can see that there are really different ... Thermal comfort is also a very subjective/cultural matter. So, I also added two custom zones that you can define using the CBE thermal comfort tool.

Let me know.

Eric

 

EDIT : to install this module :
   1. unzip the attached archive into the driectory xxx/emoncms/Modules
   2. Log in your emoncms server as admin.
   3. Go in the admin section (top right corner)
   4. Click on the "Update and check" button.
