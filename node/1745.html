Re: Problem with rfm12b on emonGLCD.

Submitted by Robert Wall on Thu, 27/12/2012 - 21:36.

The only suggestion I've seen for desoldering is to get a very large soldering iron and manufacture a custom bit that will span across the module and touch all 14 joints together. See here for an idea, but no promises that it'll work: http://forum.jeelabs.net/node/619  Also read this thread.

I've just had a thought: If you can get hold of some very thin stainless steel shim, it might be possible to slide it into each melted joint at a time. Solder won't stick to stainless steel. I haven't and don't intend to try this!

I think Glyn & Trystan's help is in order.
