Quite possibly! but not definitely, I was just suggesting a quick visual check you could do to narrow the search. No RFM activity light suggests problem is in emonTH where as a normal flashing activity light flashing once every so often (~60secs) could mean something external to emonTH.

If emonTx and emonTH were at opposite ends of house and rfm2pi in the middle you could quite easily upset one but not the other.
