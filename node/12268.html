Submitted by TrystanLea on Mon, 29/02/2016 - 18:03.

What are you using to record the pulses? if its OEM hardware or arduino/raspberrypi then you could accumulate the watt hours on the ardunio/raspberrypi, it will reset from time to time if the power is lost but you can correct for this using the wh_accumulator input process in emoncms.

The advantage of this approach for you will be that its less sensitive to internet connection or radio comm issues.
