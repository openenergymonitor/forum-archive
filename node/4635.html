Re: 3 days delay of graph drawing in emoncms.org

Submitted by TrystanLea on Tue, 01/04/2014 - 10:23.

Ok, I think I found the bug, it only becomes apparent on feeds with long fixed time intervals. The last 2 datapoints in the feeds where not being shown, for feeds with a time interval of 5 or 10s this means the data appears to have a lag of at most 15 or 30s. But for feeds with a time interval set to 10mins it looked like a 30min lag. or 24 hours = 3 days. 

The bug is now fixed on emoncms.org, Il push a fix to the emoncms repo next.
