Re: Delay beteween send data

Submitted by Robert Wall on Fri, 27/12/2013 - 19:43.

It is your choice how often you update your data. If the quantities that you measure are changing slowly, there is no need for frequent updates, and you will lighten the load on the server and have a smaller database. On the other hand, if the quantities are changing rapidly (and you do not aggregate the data at source) then you need more frequent updates otherwise will lose detail in your record of energy consumption.
