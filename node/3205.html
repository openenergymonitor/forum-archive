Just to say thanks

Submitted by Guest on Sun, 17/11/2013 - 01:42

I have followed this website for help in a project for my job. I work in southern Wisconsin, US at a concrete company. We have several machines called tilt mixers that mix up the concrete and then pours it into trucks. They are very big drums driven by two 100hp electric motors running on 480v 3 phase.  I have used your website to make a monitoring system to monitor the amp draw on each leg on both motors. This helps us catch a problem before it wrecks one of the motors or the drive system. 

I am currently on version 2 which uses two emontx and an emonglcd with an added buzzer. I would like to thank you for all the work you have done on this system. It has been a great help to me.  I am excited about the smd version of the emontx, i am looking to make about three or four more of these and will probably wait for it to hit the store before ordering.
