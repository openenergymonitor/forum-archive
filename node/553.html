Re: emontx no signs of life! - RESOLVED

Submitted by glyn.hudson on Wed, 04/04/2012 - 13:26.

You need to select UNO under tools>board to upload to emonTx, Duemianove won't work. To work out what COM port the FTDI cable is connected to try disconnecting the USB from your computer looking at the board list under tools>serial port then re-connecting it and observing what new COM port appears under tools>serial port. Or alternatively try uploading to each com port in turn!
