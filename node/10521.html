Submitted by Robert Wall on Sat, 25/04/2015 - 23:47.

Yes, it's very simple. There aren't. Only the MCP1702 is fitted. There's provision for the 7805. Look carefully at the photo's, and you'll see empty holes filled with solder.

Preempting the next question, the same is done with the burden resistors. There's provision for your choice of wire-ended resistor should you wish to change the sensitivity of the input.
