Re: problems when using multigraph

Submitted by Paul Reed on Mon, 03/02/2014 - 20:18.

Try this Eric,

  • Go to Vis and load up the multigraph that you have used in the dashboard.
  • In the multigraph preview on the right hand side, select the default view that you want to see in the dashboard. For example click 'D'
  • Save the Multigraph.

Now every time you open the dashboard you should see 1 day's worth of data by default.

In respect of the disappearing feeds, could it be that you are zooming in too far? You will need at least 2 feed datapoints on screen for a line to be drawn, and if your feeds update less frequently, or you have missing datapoints, it's easy to zoom in and have just one datapoint in view - and therefore there would be no feed evident in the graph.

Paul
