Re: AC - AC adapter for Australia

Submitted by Robert Wall on Wed, 15/01/2014 - 17:00.

The important parameter is the fidelity of the output waveform, i.e. freedom from saturation and the associated amplitude and phase distortion, and the data sheets are unlikely to tell you that. You require about 11 V out at whatever your local mains voltage is, and given that the adapter is essentially operating on no-load, a 9 V output is usually about right. The supplier we use does not appear to offer the same unit with an Australian plug.

Re: AC - AC adapter for Australia

Submitted by Robert Wall on Sun, 06/03/2016 - 14:09.

As far as I know, the actual values are not picked up from the emonhub.conf file, because I don't think there is a mechanism for the 328P to read data from the emonPi on demand. You can do it in two ways, either edit the sketch, compile it and load it as per the instructions in the Wiki, or you can (in emonhub.conf) adjust the "scales" for that node and inputs (remember power as well as voltage will be affected) by the requisite factor.
