Re: How many emonTX can I use?

Submitted by TrystanLea on Wed, 05/02/2014 - 11:49.

I have 16 nodes here (2x full emontx's and 14x low power temperature nodes). The maximum number of nodes you can have on a group is 32 (0-31) (although its 30 for normal use) node id 31 is special as it can communicate with nodes on any network and ID 0 is reserved for OOK use.

The most important thing though is that the all nodes don't transmit at the same time as they will interfere with each other. At the moment there is nothing to stop them from doing this, when there are only a few nodes the probability of collisions is small but as the number increases and the post rate increases the probability of collisions gets higher.
