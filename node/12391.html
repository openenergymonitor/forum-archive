Submitted by glyn.hudson on Sat, 26/03/2016 - 19:18.

Yup, it's possible. We've done it :-) 

Here is the example firmware for RFM69Pi + Pulse counting:

https://github.com/openenergymonitor/RFM2Pi/tree/master/firmware/RFM69CW...

You will need to compile and upload, see wiki instructions:

https://wiki.openenergymonitor.org/index.php/RFM12Pi_V2

You will need to wire pulse counter into INT1. Take a look at the RFM69Pi schematic : 

https://github.com/openenergymonitor/Hardware/tree/master/RFM2Pi/board/R...
