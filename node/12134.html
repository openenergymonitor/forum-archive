I cannot help you with the questions about the Raspberry Pi - there are others here who know far more about that.

The number of temperature sensors that you can connect to the emonTx depends heavily on the way they are connected and on the lengths of the cables. There is an article in Resources > Building Blocks about the DS18B20, and links to further information that goes into great detail about this.

Of course, you will need to modify the sketch if you want to read more than 6, and there is a limit also on the length of the data that can be transmitted by the radio link.
