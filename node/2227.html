Re: Wattcher Energy Meter and NanodeRF it's Possible ?

Submitted by Robert Wall on Mon, 15/04/2013 - 12:53.

Your first problem is to prove that the numbers you are seeing really are coming from your Wattcher. Can you change the numbers by doing things to it? - like making and stopping pulses? If you take the battery out, do the numbers stop? Until you have found that out, anything else you even think about is a waste of time.

Now I am guessing: If I wanted to design something that could see a induction disc meter going round or a LED in an electronic meter flashing, I would use light - and probably infra-red. If you put your Wattcher in a dark place with nothing in front of it, what happens? If you put some white paper in front of it to reflect any light back, what happens? These are the sort of experiments you must do to find out what the numbers mean.
