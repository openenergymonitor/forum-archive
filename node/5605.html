Input processing is a huge topic and is discussed in great detail throughout the forum. Everyone wants something a little different and the available processes also evolve, so documentation is thinner than it could be.

I'm of the opinion the fastest way to learn is to experiment and make some mistakes, working out why you didn't get the result you wanted will teach you far more than reading a guide.

The forums search facility is very useful once you pick up a few key words to focus your searches and you can always ask questions, many of us are only to happy to help if we can.
