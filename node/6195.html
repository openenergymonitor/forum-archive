Re: Measuring two enthalpy and one temperature values syncronously

Submitted by Robert Wall on Sun, 30/11/2014 - 22:20.

I'm afraid that question is a little like "how long is a piece of string?". Without knowing how you intend to measure the quantities, it is very hard to know where to begin. I suggest you browse the site and get an idea of what can be measured with the OEM kit, then see how that fits in with what you have and what instrumentation you might be able to add. On the face of it, all those measurements might be possible, but so much depends on the details.
In terms of output, I cannot recollect anyone writing the data into a spreadsheet, normally all the data ends up in emonCMS, which uses a variety of MySQL-type databases to store the data. Again, much depends on where your database is hosted.
