Submitted by ngbod on Sat, 22/03/2014 - 21:08

I just tried to swap from v2 to v3 hardware but the inputs are not in the same order. ie. voltage is coming up on the old ct2 input field and ct3 data is coming in on the original voltage field etc.

Is there a way of rearranging the inputs such as editing the key numbers on the input page?

I'm using emoncms v8.01 on rpi with emonTxV3_continuous_kwhtotals_noeeprom firmware on the emontx.

Re: changed from emontx v2 to v3 now inputs jumbled up

Submitted by Paul Reed on Sat, 22/03/2014 - 21:13.

The easiest way is to delete your 'log to Feed' process within your input page, and add a new 'log to feed' process directing the input to your correct & respective feed.

The alternative is to re-program the emontx v3 to send the cvs string in a different order, as the keys are  assigned by emoncms in the order that the data is received.

Paul
