Re: Raspberry boot problem with the downloaded image: emoncmsraspberrypihdd_stack.tar.gz (791Mb),

Submitted by Bill Thomson on Sun, 22/12/2013 - 02:30.

The error message you're getting indicates your Pi isn't recognizing your USB hard disk drive. If you are attempting to power the hard drive from the Pi USB port, the drive may be a larger load than the USB port/Pi power supply can handle. If you haven't already done so, try powering your hard drive from a separate supply.

Regards,

Bill
