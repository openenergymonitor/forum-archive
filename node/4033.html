Submitted by Paul Reed on Sun, 09/03/2014 - 22:10.

Yes. Try adding the following processes to your 'mains in' input.

Log to feed  (normal mains feed)
Allow negative (stops positive values from being logged)
Log to feed (exporting energy feed)

For further reading on processes, see - http://emoncms.org/site/docs/inputprocessing

Paul
