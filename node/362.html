Re: new emonTX pcb

Submitted by glyn.hudson on Wed, 18/01/2012 - 18:27.

CT1 and CT2 channels look like they have been assembled Ok. The the colours on the voltage divider resistors R2 and R3 for channel CT 2 look a bit odd but I think its just a different shade of violet. They should be 470K which is yellow, violet, yellow, gold/silver. If in doubt you can always check the resistance with a multimeter. 

The image on the page details which components do what: http://openenergymonitor.org/emon/node/234

CT3 is missing the 15 ohm burden resistor and 10uF capacitor. But CT1 and CT2 should still work. 

So basicly it should work OK. Can you post a photo of how you have clipped the CT round the AC cable? and maybe a shot of the underside of the board?
