Submitted by Robert Wall on Fri, 22/04/2016 - 20:51.

The problem with "debouncing" at emoncms (either local or emoncms.org) is the difficulty of knowing the time between pulses, because if the message transporting a pulse is delayed, does it make two pulses look like contact bounce or (in the other direction) contact bounce look like two pulses? 

I appreciate your desire to not change the sketch, but I think following Eric's advice is the only sure way.
