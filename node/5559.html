Re: Log Kwhd Imported and Exported (not Nett)

Submitted by Robert Wall on Thu, 14/08/2014 - 22:07.

Yes, it's been answered before. You need to "allow positive" then "Log to feed" to record the import, then "reset to zero" to kill the positive, restore the original with "+ input", then "allow negative" and "Log to feed" to record the export. If you want to display a positive value for export, multiply by -1 before you log it to the feed.
