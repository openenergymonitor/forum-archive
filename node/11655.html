Re: Dashboard dial min value

Submitted by Bill Thomson on Mon, 23/11/2015 - 23:28.

Hi Bart,

The emoncms "allow positive" parameter will enable you to log only positive data. It's on the same menu you chose "log to feed" from. After you add it to the process list, move it to the position in the process list where you want to filter out the negative values.
