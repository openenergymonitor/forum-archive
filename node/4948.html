Submitted by TrystanLea on Mon, 07/04/2014 - 16:13.

Petrik, the graph day/week/month/year button's autofade when you move the mouse cursor outside of the graph area, if you click on the graph area or move your cursor back in they should re-appear.

Brian, I think what your seeing is the effect of averaging when you zoom out, if you zoom right in it should show either 0.1 or 1.1. Your probably seeing this now as averaging is being properly handled by the phptimestore engine now. The new PHPFINA (Fixed interval no averaging) feed engine type has no averaging built in which would be ideal for recording pump states and so on.
