Hi Yoch,

The 6 inputs are:

Power 1 - from CT1
Power 2 - from CT2
Power 3 - from CT3
Power 4 - from CT4
Vrms (x10x) - mains AC voltage - only if AC adapter is connected (x 0.01 in emoncms)
Temperature (x10) - zero if no DS18B20 sensor is detected at power up (x 0.1 in emoncms)
