Submitted by Robert Wall on Fri, 03/01/2014 - 14:30.

I can't answer the stock question - Trystan & Glyn usually keep an eye on the forums but if it's urgent, email the shop.

The V3 comes pre-assembled, so you should not have any construction worries. That also makes it a little harder to customise and expand should you wish to, but on the plus side the performance at very low powers does seem to be improved. And as you say, it does not need the separate power supply. The purists will argue that the measured wave is distorted (which it is), but the amount of distortion is so small that it's impossible to detect in practical measurements.
