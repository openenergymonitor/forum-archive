I do not think I believe that. Are you sure your meter gives out a 24 V d.c. pulse? It is much more likely that the meter has a switch - which could be a relay but is more usually an opto-isolated transistor - that is capable of switching up to 50 mA at up to 24 V d.c.

If you read in Resources > Building Blocks, you will find some information, but you will find much more if you search these forums for "gas meter" and "water meter".
