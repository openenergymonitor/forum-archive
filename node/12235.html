If you zoom in on the default feed viewer in emoncms to a point where the view interval is 5 to 10 seconds and skip missing is unticked, then click on show csv you should be able to see the missing slots (shown as null) where there should otherwise be datapoints (assuming your nodes are recording at 10s) Here's a screenshot of the CSV view
