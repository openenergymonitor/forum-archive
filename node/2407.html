Re: emonTX mounting posts

Submitted by Robert Wall on Sat, 25/05/2013 - 14:36.

I used 2 mm nylon bolts and 3 mm spacers. Don't be tempted to open out the hole and use 3 mm bolts, the components are too close to the hole get a 3mm nut on.

»

Re: emonTX mounting posts

Submitted by calypso_rae on Sat, 25/05/2013 - 16:35.

If you carefully open out the holes towards the edge of the board using a needle file, you should be OK.  With component leads close by, nylon mountings are a good idea.  I find that a 3mm threaded spacer is easier to use than a nut.
