Re: make emoncms remember username....

Submitted by TrystanLea on Mon, 22/09/2014 - 14:39.

The remember me feature works by storing a cookie (with an expire time of 1 week), when it works you shouldn't see the login screen at all, when you navigate to your emoncms install it should go straight to the user profile page. Because of the way the login screen uses ajax, i couldnt get the automatic entry of username and password that many browser allow, the remember me feature is meant to be a better alternative.

Lionel, JBecker, are cookies turned off on your browsers, I dont know if IE handles them differently but could be something there
