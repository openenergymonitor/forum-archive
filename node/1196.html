Re: Creating Multigraph in emoncms.org ?

Submitted by Petrik on Tue, 23/10/2012 - 19:48.

Lets also document one important thing here, what to do in case the feeds list for multigraph is not updating. In that case you just log in to your emoncms.org and copy/paste the following to your browser: http://emoncms/vis/multigraph?clear=1 this resets the multigraph and nicely shows the current feeds list.
