List of 'things' that have been measured with Open Energy Monitor?

Submitted by Vster on Tue, 22/12/2015 - 00:09

Obviously lots of different people are measuring lots of different 'things' with official and unofficial OEM modules, but there doesn't appear to be a big list/directory somewhere where all these 'things' are listed. 

We've all done the 'usual', electricity usage and house temperature, some have gone further to monitor the output of their solar PV systems and heat pumps. I monitor the power going to my electric car charger (seemed like a good idea & it's next to the grid meter and the PV meter).

Monitoring the hallway and landing temperature is pretty standard, then it's interesting to have the outside temperature too. 

I also monitor the temperature inside my rain water harvesting tank - it freezes during very cold weather and destroys the pump. 

Last week I was wondering what others were monitoring and today I came across a chap who has temperature sensors in the flow/return pipes of his gas boiler to monitor efficiency - so I've add that to the list.

But I was wonder what else I've missed that others are doing?

(Just list them - we can start separate threads to discuss them :)
