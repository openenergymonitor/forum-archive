It does indeed sound like a supply problem.

1.You did reverse the two capacitors in the voltage pump?

2. Is it a common/earth/grounding problem - have you got a bad or missing connection, or a duplicated one that's injecting noise?

3. Are your USB plug adapter 5 V supplies clean or noisy? (There's more about that somewhere on this site).

4. Have you read this: http://openenergymonitor.org/emon/node/679
