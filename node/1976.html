Submitted by boelle on Tue, 09/04/2013 - 11:12.

specs for it:

The EA DOG series displays and backlights has been specially developed for a +3.0V and +3.3V voltage supply. Unlike other displays this series allows you to select the display type and the backlight illumination.  This way many combinations and personalized effects can be created with standard components. Simply clip the display and the backlight unit together and you have up to 63 possible combinations. Currently the graphic DOG displays are available in 128x64 and 128x32 pixel and also as alphanumeric display 1x8, 2x16 or 3x16 lines. 

Each type comes in STN yellow/green, STN reflective, STN negative blue, FSTN blue, FSTN positive and negative. There is also a choice of 5 different backlights including a full RGB backlight.  See selection table or simulator software below.

Features

  • Chip-on-Glass technology (STN and FSTN)
  • LED backlight in various colours
  • Graphic DOG 128x64 and 128x32 pixel or 1x8, 2x16 and 3x16 (Lines x Characters) 
  • Very flat: 2.0 mm (display only) and 5.8 mm with backlight
  • Controller ST7565  with SPI interface
  • Easy SPI interface. Only control 4 pins. 
  • +3.0V or +3.3V Supply Voltage (Typical. 150 micro Amp)
  • No negative voltage required
  • Operating temperature -20 to +70 °C
  • No temperature compensation necessary
  • Easy mounting, solder direct into PCB or use with socket FL20
  • Ideal for use in handheld and portable applications
  • Personal design effects with short delivery times
