Re: NOOB - openenergymonitor without Emonglcd but with an android tablet?

Submitted by Lloyd on Wed, 17/10/2012 - 19:45.

You certainly can build your system without the LCD display - the only thing that you will lose is the internal room temperature.  

Basically emontx is used to read consumption, and this is transmitted to emonbase (for upload to emoncms) and/or emonlcd (for display).  The server software (emoncms) does the per day calculation for the web based display, and emonlcd does it own calculation for its own display.

 

Lloyd
