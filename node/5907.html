Does the LED activity change on the emonTx ? if the led pulses with successful transmissions and continues when the transmissions are no longer received that could point to an issue with the RFM12B or range/interference/collisions etc. 

Have you tried temporarily moving the Pi and Tx closer together and stopping the TH just to rule out range/interference/collisions etc?
