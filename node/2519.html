Re: Emontx enclosure - How do you fit the AA batteries?

Submitted by Paul Reed on Mon, 01/07/2013 - 19:29.

I think that the short answer is that they don't fit!

It looks as though the enclosures were intended for a PP3 - 9V battery, ie with both connections on the same side, and it's extremely tight to fit 2 x AA batteries in there. As you have already noticed, you can see the case bulging in the photo top left.

I cut a small length of stainless steel plate, bent the two edges up slightly, and hot glued it to the plastic case, to provide a connection between the + & - of the AA batteries.

Works, but again it distorts the case. 

Paul
