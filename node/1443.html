Submitted by ukmoose on Tue, 27/11/2012 - 09:05.

As emoncms.org is a public website you shouldn't use the IP address. 

The company hosting it could change the server it is hosted on and break your script.

 

Try changing your dns server settings on your router or in the script to either google's public dns servers 8.8.8.8 & 8.8.4.4

or those provided by opendns.com (others are available)
