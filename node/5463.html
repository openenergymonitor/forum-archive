To get  a similar voltage level at the RFM12B, I would reduce the 4K7 resistors to 680 ohms or maybe 560 ohms.

(10 / (10 + 0.68 )) * 3.6 = 3.37  // 680R with a 3.6V supply

(10 / (10 + 4.7 )) * 5 = 3.40     // 4K7 with a 5V supply

(10 / (10 + 0.56 )) * 3.6 = 3.41  // 560R with a 3.6V supply
