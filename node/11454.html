Re: not possible to save new dashboard configuration

Submitted by Paul Reed on Thu, 22/10/2015 - 20:14.

V9.0 introduces a change in the way emoncms handles timezones, which if not set up correctly can influence the retrieval of 'daily' feeds.

I've just had a quick look at emoncms.org (I don't use it myself) and noticed that Setup>MyAccount>Timezone is unpopulated, so it's not possible to select your timezone - presumably a bug.

I wouldn't delete your daily feeds - you may find that the data is safely there, and retrievable once the update issues are sorted.

 

Paul
