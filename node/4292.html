Hi Glyn,

Just wanted to pass along what I know of Tapatalk. We used it on the Aluminumalloyboats.com forum (OEM's USA host) and it attracted spam to some degree. We now use a php script called ZBBlock, and our spam problem has disappeared completely. The author of BBlock comments here, why Tapatalk isn't secure: www.spambotsecurity.com/forum/viewtopic.php?f=32&t=2430

It's the 2nd post on the page.

Another at: www.spambotsecurity.com/forum/viewtopic.php?f=9&t=1864 (last post on the page)

and one more at: www.spambotsecurity.com/forum/viewtopic.php?f=32&t=2430  (quote at the bottom of the page)

Oh, almost forgot, ZBBlock is free, and IS available for Drupal...
