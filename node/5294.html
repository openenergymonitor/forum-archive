Migrating to Emoncms : I like it

Submitted by Charly86 on Wed, 11/06/2014 - 16:18

Hi there,

Just a small presentation, I'm collecting data for years (temp, humidity, power, weather, ..) into mySQL database and I'm switching all my datas to emoncms.

I'm using some custom Arduino Nodes, Fibaro Home Automation, RFMXCom to collect datas thru FHEM, Raspberry Pi, Sheevaplug, OpenWrt device, well lot's of geek device and I want to consolidate to avoid multiple systems.

I would like to thanks all creators, this is a really great product and I'm really happy with it, it's open and customizable, fast, and I have one account onto your main emoncms site plus one other on a clouded VM with latest repo.

This is not a brilliant post, just to say I love this emoncms software ;-)

Of course I got lot of questions, problems, ... but I will ask each on a dedicated post to avoid multiple question on one post.
