Re: Arduino energy monitor how connect CT sensor and AC Adapter ??

Submitted by Robert Wall on Wed, 10/12/2014 - 21:37.

The ac-ac adapter and the CT are manufactured with the plugs fitted. You can either buy suitable sockets (like those on the emonTx) to suit those plugs, then use ordinary single-core wire to connect the socket to your Arduino, or you can cut off the plugs and make the connection directly.
